Facility & Challenge

W Abrasives operates a temper shot retort furnace in Ontario, Canada, used in abrasive shot manufacturing. The furnace is 17' long and 48" in diameter, fired by 9 burners at 450,000 BTU total rated capacity, with an insulating firebrick (IFB) lining.

Challenges identified:

  • Refractory maintenance costs running $10,000/year — IFB degradation requiring continual repair
  • Energy consumption at 325 kWh/ton — above optimal efficiency
  • High CO₂ emissions from energy-intensive process — 940 tonnes CO₂/year
  • Extended preheat times reducing productive operating hours

ITC Solution

ITC 100HT

ITC 100HT was applied to the hot face of the IFB lining throughout the retort furnace. The coating seals and protects the IFB surface, reflects radiant heat back into the process zone, and eliminates the primary degradation mechanisms that drive refractory replacement.
