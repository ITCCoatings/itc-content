Company Overview

voestalpine AG is an Austrian-headquartered steel and technology group with EUR 16.7 billion in revenue, 51,600 employees, and 500+ locations in 50+ countries. The group operates four divisions: Steel, High Performance Metals, Metal Engineering, and Metal Forming.

ITC Relationship: First successful trial completed at the Kapfenberg forge furnace (High Performance Metals Division). voestalpine is now looking to expand ITC coatings to additional locations.

This document identifies ITC coating applications across voestalpine's major production facilities worldwide.

Facility 1: Linz Integrated Steelworks (Steel Division HQ)

Location: Linz, Austria | Capacity: 6 million tonnes crude steel/year | Type: Fully integrated steelworks

voestalpine's flagship facility and Austria's largest industrial plant. Includes coking, sintering, blast furnaces, BOF converters, continuous casting, hot rolling, cold rolling, and galvanizing.

Coking Plant

Austria's only coking plant. Produces 1.3 million tonnes of coke/year (blast furnaces require 1.9M tonnes -- remainder sourced externally).

Coke Oven Doors and Chamber Walls

ITC 100HT ITC 296A

Thermal shock protection on oven door refractory and silica brick chamber walls. Extends oven battery campaign life and reduces emissions from door seal degradation.

Sinter Plant

Capacity expanded 30% with new waste-gas recycling system. Only sinter strand in Europe with SCR deNOx catalyst.

Sinter Plant Ductwork and Anchors

ITC 213 ITC 100HT ITC 296A

Three-coat system on steel ductwork prevents acid dew point corrosion. ITC 213 on refractory anchors prevents alkali corrosion.

Reference: TXI Cement bag house (4-5x shell life) + Anchor Corrosion Testing (100% improvement rate)

Blast Furnaces (A, 5, 6)

Three blast furnaces producing 5 million tonnes pig iron/year. BF-A is the most powerful at 3.3M tonnes/year. BF-5 and BF-6 combined produce ~1.6M tonnes.

Hot Blast Stoves

ITC 100HT ITC 296A

Coating on stove dome and upper checker bricks protects refractory from thermal cycling, reduces heat loss, and improves blast temperature efficiency.

Blast Furnace Tuyere Area and Cast House Runners

ITC 213 ITC 100HT

ITC 213 on metal components for oxidation/erosion protection. ITC 100HT on runner refractory extends campaign life.

Torpedo Cars / Hot Metal Ladles

ITC 213

Ceramic coating on steel shell interior prevents molten iron breakouts by blocking carbon diffusion into the steel shell. Safety-critical application.

Reference: Molten Iron Breakout Prevention tech bulletin

BOF Steelmaking (3 Converters)

Three BOF converters (177 tonnes each) producing 6M tonnes crude steel/year.

BOF Off-Gas Ductwork and Converter Hoods

ITC 213 ITC 100HT

Protection against extreme temperature oxidation and deposit buildup on water-cooled and refractory-lined components.

Steel Ladle Shells and Covers

ITC 213 ITC 100HT

Breakout prevention on ladle shells. Ceramic fiber protection on ladle covers extends life 3-5x.

Reference: McConway and Torley (3-5x refractory life, 46% temperature uniformity improvement)

Continuous Casting (CC8 + others)

CC8 produces 1.2M tonnes of high-quality slabs/year. Multiple casters for slab production.

Tundish Covers, Shells, and Preheaters

ITC 100HT ITC 213

Fiber protection on tundish covers. Breakout prevention on tundish shells. Improved heat retention for casting quality.

Hot Rolling Mill (5M tonnes/year capacity)

Reheat Furnaces

ITC 100HT ITC 296A

Veneer and coating system on furnace roof, sidewalls, and skid pipes reduces BTU loss, lowers shell temperatures, and extends refractory life.

Reference: Nucor Steel ($1.23M/yr fuel savings), Arcelor Mittal ($3.76M/yr total impact)

Cold Rolling and Galvanizing

Bell Annealers (H2 atmosphere, EBNER HICON/H2)

ITC 100HT

ITC 100HT on interior refractory/fiber lining of bell annealer covers and bases protects against thermal cycling and improves temperature uniformity.

Reference: McConway and Torley (46% temperature uniformity improvement)

Galvanizing Line Furnaces (Direct-fired and Radiant Tube)

ITC 100HT ITC 213

ITC 100HT on furnace refractory for energy efficiency. ITC 213 on radiant tubes and metallic components for oxidation protection and life extension.
